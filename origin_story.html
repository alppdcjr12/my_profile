    Where do we begin?

    For me, I like to count my beginning as the moment my journey began. The moment it began in earnest.

    Sometimes it feels like that wasn't until I started to understand my place in things: That my life is a continuation of the energy of the earth and the universe and that I am just as conditioned by cause and effect as any phenomenon that can be named.

    In a more relative sense, there are a thousand starting points, each that pushes me in a slightly different direction but that culminate ultimately in a broad trajectory which I feel points to a unifying, underlying purpose.

    What are these starting points?

    Growing up and learning--being taught--that I was worth something and had something to give.

    Going off to college. Making friends. Learning to trust myself. Getting stranded in a farway city and sleeping on a park bench.

    Couch surfing. Learning a new language. Traveling abroad.

    Committing to getting better as a writer. Throwing myself into self-development. Learning to code.

    And the journey continues...
